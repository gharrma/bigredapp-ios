a café/coffee shop in Olin Library


Located in Olin Library, Amit Bhatia Libe Café is the perfect place to take a study break, or to enjoy a latte and a pastry while you enjoy wireless Internet access on your laptop.

A combined effort between Cornell Dining and Olin Library, the Amit Bhatia Libe Café serves specialty Starbucks coffees, smoothies, pastries, and Freshtake Grab-n-Go sandwiches, salads, and snacks.

Sunday through Wednesday nights the café stays open until midnight to accommodate late-night studiers.
